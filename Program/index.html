Rise and Shine Together


We are an established primary school located in the heart of the township. Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for our students to learn and grow. We strive to create a learning environment that is both stimulating and supportive, and to foster a sense of community among our students, staff, and families. At our school, we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to succeed. We strive to provide a comprehensive education.
